A Healthy Happy You

The way you feel physically and mentally can impact your life. Taking care of your body will not only make you feel good, but also make you happier. In today's world, there are many processed foods and junk that make people feel awful. It is paramount that you learn about the foods that will make you feel your best.

Living a healthier lifestyle is mostly dependent on what you are consuming. However a sedentary lifestyle is not ideal and will not always make you feel as good as you should. There are many ways to exercise that are fun and exciting!

  1. Go for a 30 min walk/run
  2. Take a zumba class, where you and your friends can dance and laugh!
  3. Join a sport that your classmates are involved in
  4. Go biking around your neighborhood
  5. Go to www.youtube.com where there are a variety of different work out videos you can do at home without any equipment

Working out does not have to be a dreaded activity! There are ways to have fun and burn some calories!

What does being Healthy mean?

Being healthy should not be having the same body as your favorite celebrity or starving yourself everyday to lose weight. You should eat until you are satisfied, but eat the foods that are healthy and will promote a sustainable amazing lifestyle. For example, 400 calories worth of chips will not make you feel as good as 400 calories worth of fruit or vegetables

Healthy carbs such as potatoes, rice, and fruit will help to shed excess fat. Your cells run on glucose and seek the sugar that is inside of these carbs. There are many unhealthy carbs in chips, cookies, and junk foods. These should be avoided.

It is okay to have foods that are high in fat or not healthy once in a while. You never want to deprive yourself from a food that you genuinely enjoy. However, eat any food in moderation. Whether it is healthy or not, do not eat when you are not hungry.
